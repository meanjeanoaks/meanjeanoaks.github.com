The Mean Jean Oaks began recording their self-titled debut late summer 2015 in their practice space in Williamsburg, Brooklyn. For three months, the Oaks crew tried to capture the raw energy of their live show and experimental jam sessions. The album is more song focused than the initial free-form improvisation of the Mean Jean Oaks beginings and it marks the first true release of a band that has been around for quite some time and has seen lineups as large as seven musicieans to the current lineup as a three-piece.
